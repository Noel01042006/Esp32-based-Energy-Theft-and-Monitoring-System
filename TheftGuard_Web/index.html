System Guide

Your complete guide to monitoring your grid and detecting power theft.

1. How Theft Detection Works

The system constantly compares the Source (power entering the grid) with the Load (power legitimately consumed by the house). If Source > Load by more than 0.2A, a Critical Red Alert banner triggers instantly, indicating a potential bypass or physical line tap.

2. Dual Analytics (Load vs. Source)

The dashboard features two independent charts. The top blue chart maps your House Load, while the bottom yellow chart maps the Grid Source.

3. Cost & Budgeting

Navigate to the Cost tab to view your live session bill.

4. Hardware & Settings

Click Pair Device to link your ESP32. Navigate to the Control tab to physically disconnect the load relay.
